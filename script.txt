Before humanity reached the stars, something else was already out there. Ancient. Godlike. Gone.

They called themselves the Forerunners — a civilization so advanced they built megastructures the size of worlds. But they faced something that couldn't be reasoned with. Something that turned life itself into a weapon. They called it the Flood. A parasitic intelligence with one goal: consume all sentient life in the galaxy. The Forerunners fought back. And lost. So in their final act of desperation, they built the Halo Array — seven massive ring-shaped superweapons, each capable of wiping out all sentient life in the galaxy. Not to destroy the Flood. To starve it. No hosts. No food. No Flood.

Then they fired it. And they were gone too.

One hundred thousand years later... humanity stumbled into the war of its life.
